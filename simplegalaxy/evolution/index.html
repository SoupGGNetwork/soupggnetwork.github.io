  • Major Database Attack 24 June 2018

    ?? IMPORTANT INFORMATION ??
    Hackers have compromised our database and deleted all data (including player stats, ranks, etc.).
    There are no database backups.

    It cannot be ruled out that they have also stolen sensitive data such as e-mail addresses and encrypted passwords.

    — SimpleGalaxy Network (@SimpleGalaxyNET) 24.June 2018

    It is not known who the attackers are. It can be assumed that a bot that automatically scans servers for unprotected databases (ransomware) has found and compromised our database.

    The problem is that no firewall has been set up and database authentication was/is still disabled.

    — SimpleGalaxy Network (@SimpleGalaxyNET) 24 June 2018

    This message (attached screenshot) was left by the ransomware.

    Unfortunately, this is not uncommon - it is indeed a criminal system.
    This is most likely a scam because paying the ransom usually does not result in receiving the backup, as none is created at all. pic.twitter.com/wqBUxwKMXY

    — SimpleGalaxy Network (@SimpleGalaxyNET) 24 June 2018

    In case someone uses the password of his SimpleGalaxy account for other services too, it is strongly advised to change it there.

    — SimpleGalaxy Network (@SimpleGalaxyNET) 24 June 2018
